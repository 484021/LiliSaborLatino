Welcome to the Culinary World of Liliana Duque Munoz

Embark on a Delectable Journey with Chef Liliana Duque Munoz

A dedicated culinary virtuoso hailing from the vibrant city of Cali, Colombia.

Exquisite Catering Services

At the heart of Chef Liliana's culinary artistry lies a commitment to creating unforgettable gastronomic experiences. Whether it's an intimate gathering, a corporate event, or a lavish celebration, Chef Liliana curates tailor-made menus that delight the senses and elevate every occasion. Her culinary creations are a harmonious blend of locally sourced ingredients, culinary innovation, and a deep respect for time-honored traditions.

Culinary Mastery

Chef Liliana's expertise extends beyond the boundaries of conventional cuisine, transcending flavors and textures to present a symphony of gastronomic wonders. With an unwavering dedication to excellence, she orchestrates an unforgettable culinary journey, redefining the benchmarks of taste and presentation.

Bespoke Dining Experiences

Dedicated to delivering unparalleled excellence, Chef Liliana curates bespoke dining experiences, each exuding an aura of sophistication and indulgence. From elegant plated dinners to interactive culinary showcases, every moment becomes a celebration of the art of food.

  • Our Mission

    At Lili & Paul's Food Truck and catering , our mission is to bring wholesome, clean, and artisanal food made from scratch to the streets. We are dedicated to serving dishes crafted with love and care, using only the freshest ingredients—never anything from a packet. Our passion for food drives us to provide a memorable and heartfelt culinary experience, one plate at a time.

  • Our Vision

    Our vision is to become a beloved fixture in the community, known for our commitment to quality and authenticity. We aspire to create a space where people can enjoy delicious, home-cooked meals that nourish both the body and soul. Through our food truck and catering we aim to spread joy, foster connections, and celebrate the art of cooking from the heart. Connecting South America to Canada.
